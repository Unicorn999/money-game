The money game

1. You have rupees 50 2. To win you need 100 rupees 3. To lose you need 0 rupees 4. Choose between button X, button Y and button Z 5. One button halves (e.g- 5O becomes 25) 6. The other minuses 5 rupees (e.g- 5O becomes 45) 7. The other increases your amount by the amount of money you have (e.g- 5O becomes 100)
