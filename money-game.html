The money game

Rules

  1. You have rupees 40
  2. To win you need 100 rupees
  3. To lose you need 0 rupees
  4. Choose between button X, button Y and button Z
  5. One button halves (e.g- 4O becomes 20)
  6. The other minuses 10 rupees (e.g- 4O becomes 30)
  7. The other doubles the money you have (e.g 4O becomes 80)

You have: 40 Rs
